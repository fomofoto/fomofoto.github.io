Partecipa
 
Diventa una collaboratrice o un collaboratore di fomofoto per avere la possibilità di essere pubblicato su Vogue Italia

Sono un fotografo emergente mi chiamo Antonio e sono qui per proporti collaborazioni professionali se sei una maodella/o motivata a sfondare nel mondo del fashion e, ovviamente, punti in alto, esattamente come me!

Ti propongo di creare un book fotografico in collaborazione con me perchè, sono tra i migliori emergenti e punto alle selezioni di testate come Vogue Italia; insieme potremo creare progetti fantastici, che arricchiranno il tuo ed il mio portfolio; tutti i progetti degni di nota saranno segnalati alle migliori testate fashion come Vogue.

Only this and an impeccable composition can make the difference in a portrait created by a professionist, location, project, model and type of portrait are only secondary things.

Our method

Everything starts from a customer request, as specific as possible, with a clear goal to reach, to give me the opportunity to give the best of me.

This is followed by a phase of designing the shooting where we will agree together some details based on the budget among which, type of project, model, emotions to instill in the portrait, location and more..

Immediately afterwards, the actual service will be performed where I will exploit all the capabilities of the model in question to express the emotions required in the agreed composition.

Unfortunately for me, the work will not be limited to this, but will follow a phase of photoediting in the studio to bring the finished product on your desk, usually on a storage medium, but I also do not disdain requests for printing and layout.

How can I help you

I really like shooting in the fields of fashion, glamor, nude and urban photography.

I can help you choose the best channels where you can find models for your portraits and advise the way, budget and location according to your initial ideas if you do not have a clear project in mind yet.

Moreover, I can help you in the editorial layout of the final product both in print and on the web.

It is important, however, that you understand that this type of projects are very complicated because of the logistics organization, so, especially, I prefer to work with large brands well organized or with small companies that persue a project well done and comprehensive.
