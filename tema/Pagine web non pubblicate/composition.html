Consulting the rules of composition before taking a photograph is like consulting the laws of gravity before going for a walk.

In every composition made to perfection there is an emotion hidden inside, only after careful analysis you will be able to understand it fully, did you know?

The compositions may be able to evoke emotions and needs, evoked directly in the minds of the people to whom they are directed, because they contain powerful symbols of persuasion, I bet you knew that too, right?

For this reason compositions are very important for you, in order to create interest, authority, desirability and sympathy, in the eyes of the right people, of the right audience for your activities.
