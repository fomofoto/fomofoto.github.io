Our method

It's nice to go for a walk to find landscapes and interesting natural areas to photograph, you can have fun and enjoy yourself!

But my work does not end here, it would be nice, but you will not believe in fairy tales? I am a professional.

My real work is concentrated in the phases before and after the shooting of the landscape or of my objectives.

The preliminary phase consists in designing a meticulous plan to center the objectives given by the client of the work, assessing the best times in which to operate, in conjunction with problems related to traffic conditions and legality of the shots that I would capture.

The final phase consists in going back to the studio and starting to edit the photographs taken, which includes a selection of the best (I'm not a sniper to get perfect results you need to take lots of photos), and the edit of the same, which, otherwise they would seem flat and with little color in the final result.
