Don't be afraid of being different. Be afraid of being the same as everyone else.

I've never done a professional boxing match, I've ever participated in an Ironman. I have not even been in space. But I have traveled many far away places, created a lot of still and moving pictures for myself and others.

I chose, among the objectives of my life, that to make my creativity flow freely in everything I do.

Mission

In an isolated place like a desert island or in Antarctica, I would go crazy without photography, videomaking, without flying my drone, without the possibility of reading and without my inseparable buddy 'Papi'.

Marketing, storytelling, innovation, creativity and visual voodo - regardless of the means of communication, all this, makes my heart beat faster and makes me excited to be able to enjoy it, my soul sings of joy when it is in similar contexts.

I like to have fun, to have fun and meet new people, but only the original ones, creative and empathic, that is, those that can positively enrich my knowledge and my life.

I was transparent long before it was so fashionable, and I deeply believe in teamwork, community and collaboration. Become friends. Better yet, let's start an adventure together! A business adventure, so to speak...
